Humility

Not all structures are equal.

As the world becomes more leveraged, the opportunity vehicle we're in could be the difference between ultrawealth and poverty.

From a material perspective, the choice matters immensely. Morally, all that matter is that we choose.

The trick, as far as I can tell, is to have the humility to place the moral above the material.

We could argue about the value of different structures, but we can't deny the necessity of choosing something.

Of course, this is complex.

It's all too easy to choose an easier path, using false moral superiority as an excuse to be pathetic.

Which leads us to some important ideas.
