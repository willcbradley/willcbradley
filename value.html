Value

People aren’t stupid.

They’re not going to give us something for nothing (at least, we shouldn’t bank on it).

So, if we want something valuable from society, we need something valuable to provide in exchange.

Historically, many people have done this by getting jobs.

But when repeatable/algorithmic tasks are automated, employees’ pricing power can be eaten away.

(After all, when’s the last time you saw a rich lamp-lighter?)

Instead, we need some way of creating future-proof value, without taking our eye off the ball with today’s work.

And to do that, we first need to understand leverage.
