Structure

Thinking is expensive.

Structures (like families, disciplines, communities, and institutions) keep us from having to re-derive our values with each new decision.

This is somewhat restrictive, and can give rise to corruption. But the alternative (zero structure and complete reliance on cognitive horsepower) is enough to drive anybody insane.

But that still leaves the problem: which structure to take shelter in?
