Dedication

As of writing, I've recently turned 21. I have no wife yet, let alone kids.

I'm typing this text, on a keyboard, into the source code of a website. Who knows what format it'll be in when you come across it!

Maybe, by the time you've found this, I'll be dead. Maybe not. But either way, these ideas are the most valuable gifts I could leave you with.

They were extremely painful lessons to learn, but in retrospect, it was more than worth it. Hopefully, I can give you a head start.

Inevitably, you'll disagree with me on some things.

Most of the time, you'll be wrong. But occasionally, you'll be right, and those few occasions will make all the difference.

There's no time to waste. No shortage of problems to solve.

But if you produce more than you consume, and never stop trying to make things better, then I couldn't be prouder.

Love always,

- Dad
