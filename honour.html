Honour

The future is uncertain.

Just because things can get better, doesn't mean they'll get better in our lifetimes.

Which means we need something more durable than narrow, short term self-interest.

Acting honourably requires us to accept the constraints of a value system, despite the catastrophic risk this entails.

From a rational perspective, this makes no sense. But maybe that's the point.

Reality is cutthroat.

Without putting our lives on the line, we will not survive.
