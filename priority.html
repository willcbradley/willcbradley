Priority

Our options are endless.

At any given moment, there are infinite ways we could be spending our time.

What to do, in the face of such choice?

Simple: choose the best option.

But notice something interesting. We can't make the best choice without the existence of "better" (and therefore "worse").

If everything were perfectly equal, we'd be too overwhelmed to anything at all.

Action depends on priorities, and priorities depend on inequality.

But re-thinking these priorities every second of every day is highly inefficient. Instead, we rely on structures.
