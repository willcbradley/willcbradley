Optimism

Hopelessness kills.

If there's no chance that things will improve, sacrifice is futile.

Why forego things today, if it's only downhill from here? Why work for the good of the world, if we refuse to acknowledge the existence of "good"?

All sustainable value systems depend on the belief that tomorrow can be better than today.

But even more than that, they force us to shoulder an impossible (and necessary) burden.
